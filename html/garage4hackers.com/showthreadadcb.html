    Garage4Hackers Kashmir Relief donations Update Part I

    Garage4Hackers Kashmir Relief donations Update Part I

    2014 Kashmir Floods started at 5 September and the intensity was 22.40 feet (6.83*m) w above the danger mark
    Pressure started building up when I totally cut off in communication with my friends and family for about 5 days .

    Following are some of the devastating pictures I took during my visit over there.










    Since the second day I heard the news, I started collecting donations . And lots of good people from g4h started donating. A bug bounty challenge on the lines of ALS Bucket challenge was pitched forward. The G4H Facebook page was buzz with generous donations from many people who were from Indian infosec community and also friends of friends. I would like to thank all from the bottom of my heart ! https://www.facebook.com/Garage4Hack...e=1&permPage=1

    I would like to thank Garage4Hackers for the immense and swift support in this humanitarian effort. https://twitter.com/garage4hackers/s...07086373556224
    Also thanks to fb1h2s for starting a ALS like thing for Kashmir Donations . http://garage4hackers.com/showthread...ll=1#post13551


    On 16 of Sep we were able to buy the following general items

    Generic Medicine .

    augmentin 500
    doxycilin
    ornof
    emset
    levofloaxcin
    sinarest
    montair lc
    metformin
    throxin
    thyroxin*
    injections
    syringes
    some gloves
    hand sanitizes
    sanitary napkins for women
    betadine ointments'
    gauzes, bandages
    energy bars



    Thanks to KeepFit Medicals Stores for providing us with a huge discount

    Medical Devices

    We brought some Nebulizers also . Nebulizer is a drug delivery device used to administer medication in the form of a mist inhaled into the lungs. This was brought in to attention by authorities over there at the Srinagar airport responsible for handling and forwarding the relief material .

    Thanks for KeepFit Medicals Stores, Pune (India) for providing us with a huge discount.







    Blankets .

    We were also able to send 60 blankets . People at Maharaja Store's in AB Chowk, Pune (India) provided us the blankets with huge discounts.


    And At last I would like to personally thank Summi of Indigo Airlines for providing us Free of cost cargo consignment https://twitter.com/raashidbhatt/sta...14311432126464





    PS: I will be posting another announcement shortly after communication and banking is restored in Kashmir
