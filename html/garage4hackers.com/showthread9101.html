    Garage T-Shirts - Feb 2012

    Dear Garage Members,

    We are happy to announce that this year’s first set of Garage T-shirts is now available. We have chose black to be the base color of the t-shirts having a very “Decent” message on front side saying “Though Shalt RTFM Before Asking Questions” and our latest logo printed on the backside along with the forum URL.

    The T-shirts can be viewed and ordered from Garage Goodies’ online Pringoo shop at below link –
    Welcome to Garage 4 Hackers shop : Powered by Pringoo www.pringoo.com

    As of now, for users’ ease, we have published only one design. To order just click on “g4h official” which shall take you to the pringoo website.

    Follow the easy instructions and if you have any issues you can either RTFM or contact garage moderators. You will need to have an account at pringoo for placing the order.

    Also Discount Code “LUV12” can be used to avail 15% discount on the order amount.

    For any queries contact info[@]garage4hackers.com
