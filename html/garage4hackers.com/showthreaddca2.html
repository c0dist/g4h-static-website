    Secondly, the nopsled used in heap sprays is a little special. It has dual purpose:

    1. The nop sled must form an address that again point back to the somewhere in the nopsled in nopsled+shellcode area.

    2. Nopsled should also form the executable instructions, which are legitimate and doesnt change the state of general purpose registers OR the registers important for proper exploitation of the candidate vulnerability.

    Now let us take some examples of some candidate nopsleds:

    0x90909090 : This is good one single instruction (0x90 NOP) but has a problem. Our nop sled will also form (or lie on a location where a vftable was in past time) a vftable and the virtual function's address's will become 0x90909090. Now for proper execution, our nopsled+shellcode must exist at this location and the cpu must land on the nopsled area.
    Remember heap addresses start from lower number and are in increasing order. To achieve a spray that will spray the 0x90909090 memory block, will take huge time and may be this area will never be sprayed, so this kind of nop is not used.

    0x0C0C0C0C : This area always lie in the heap block and also 0x0C0C is an instruction with two bytes, so is a very good candidate as this address can be sprayed in a very small time.
    [ Remember the IEpeers in IE6, the effective spray time is just a flicker of our eyes].

    Note: being a dual byte instruction, you must prefix the shellcode with some single byte nopsled right after the 0x0c0c0c0c nopsled. Otherwise the odd byte (0x0c) from 0x0c0c0c0c nopsled will pairup (0x0c being dual byte instruction) with the first byte of shellcode, foiling the proper execution of shellcode.It should be like this:

    %u0c0c% u0c0c0% u0c0c% u0c0c % u9191% u9191%uShellcode bytes........

    Note : Remove spaces from above line. Otherwise, the unicode will be interpreted in a proper font.

    Similarly 0x0d0d0d0d and likewise.
    Remember the rule, the nopsled should form the proper instruction set and those instructions should not change the state of registers important for proper exploitation of the issue, and the address should lie in heap area and might be achieved by a little amount of spray.
