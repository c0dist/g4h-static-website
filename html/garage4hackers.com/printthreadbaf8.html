    ISO 27001 is for Small medium business
    ISO 27001 is for Small medium business, which requires only a minimum of procedures and technology in order to be compliant with this standard.

    This makes it all the more important that a firm's information security management should be carried out by someone with expertise and experience of both the ISO 27001 standard and the field of information security in general.

    Advantage of ISO 27001 standard
    • ISO 27001 standard map out a framework for effective security information.
    • ISO 27001 commit the organization to compliance with legal, regulatory, and statutory requirements.
    • Enhance the security awareness within an organization.
    • Avoidance of threats and vulnerabilities that affect the organization.
