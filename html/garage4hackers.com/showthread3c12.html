    Stuxnet-Under the microscope

    Hope you guys are all aware of the worm Stuxnet that has hit computers at the Bushehr nuclear power plant in Iran,for the first time there are doubts the computer worm was probably written and disseminated either by a government or a very well-funded private group and seems to be the work of a coterie of expert hackers.

    I was just researching on the behaviour and the targeted devices of the worm and am quite surprised to see for the first time a worm targetting industrial devices like SCADA systems...the first known malware specifically to target control systems at big industrial facilities such as power plants and oil refineries.The first worm to have a PLC rootkit and with digitally signed authentic certificates which makes it virtually undetectable.

    Affected countries

    A study of the spread of Stuxnet by Symantec showed that the main affected countries as of August 6, 2010 were

    Country Infected Computers

    China 6,000,000 (unconfirmed)
    Iran 62,867
    Indonesia 13,336
    India 6,552
    United States 2,913

    We stand 3rd in the count of infections,I highly recommend any tech lover to read this article, which explains the full detailed working of the malware.

    http://www.eset.com/resources/white-...Microscope.pdf
