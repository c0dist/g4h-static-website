    A Successful First Year for Garage

    "The story of computing is epic. It’s driven by the human passion for tinkering, inventing and solving difficult problems where accidents and luck can be as important as brilliant engineering"

    Many of us would have wondered why the name 'Garage' for us,number of Computer wizards/gurus had their starting at some car garage.Steve Wozniaki & Steve Jobs rise from garage to gigabyte was awe inspiring,Paul Allen & Bill Gates started building the Microsoft empire from a garage in Albuquerque and many more such cases.

    We visioned 'Garage 4 Hackers' to always be an virtual garage where wannabe hackers,security researchers can hang around and use it as a launching pad for their careers.Our aim and motto was always to help,share,guide,innovate and shape the careers of individuals who want to take this path.So lets use G4H to tinker,invent,colloborate and solve difficult problems and produce some brilliant solutions.

    For all who are new to the G4H, before G4H transforming into a full blown security community/forum in June 2010, we had a humble & slow beginning as an Orkut Community (Hackers Garage) http://www.orkut.co.in/Main#Community?cmm=26420310 wayback in Jan 2007.


    Although we enjoyed the small yet cohesive community,we felt that the time was right to launch our own forum and break the limitations of orkut to reach wider audiences so we decided that going for a CMS solution like VB would be the best way forward. Before taking the plunge we did a lot of research - vital for anyone thinking of launching a security foum - and we were really impressed by the support and encouragement of all our members.Many of our core members spend nights migrating/importing threads from Orkut to VB.

    Although things were tough at first,we started off with less than 5 active members.Today we have 800 odd members and 171 active members and really interesting discussions.I'm really confident about the future and there are very exciting times ahead and I know that the support we need will always be there from all of you and together we will make a difference.At the end of the what really gives us satisfaction is when someone says 'G4H really made a difference to my career'.


    G4H has never been about an individual and never will be,its been a team and it will always be a team.I would like to put in special thanks to my Co-admins B0nd & WiPU and Core Team members/Moderators Fb1H2s,Godwin,neo,empty,prashant,anantshri,s1ayer, Ajay,Abhay,[S],DZZ,41.w4r10r,D4rk357 who have been the backbone of G4H and our senior members like beenu,Vinnu,Hackuin,webdevil,thats_all,steve_ron who have been a real inspiration to all of us here, shoutz and cheers to Nishant,Atul,mayjune, d4rkd4wn, karniv0re, silentph33r, keval_domadia, micr0, steve_ron ,sebas_phoenix,swatantra,Boris,goldshlager, d3c0d3r,MrGrey,sandeep,sh0bhit,rishab for their valuable contributions,suggestions and support.
    Special thanks to null,prohack,AH and clubhack.Thanks to all passive members in the forum and sincere apologies to whomever I missed out.


    Above all we would like to hear from all of you,its suggestions,feedbacks,critics that make us grow.

    Cheerz
