    ISO standards in Business and society

    ISO certification benefits businesses because they can focus their resources on producing goods and services that are internationally standardized.

    ISO standards improve production by making each activity within the overall process, such as developing, manufacturing and supplying, more efficient.

    ISO Standards also play a vital role in the development of high-tech goods. When a business innovate a new technology, international standards facilitate the development of terminology and compatibility, making the innovation marketable.
