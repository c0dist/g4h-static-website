    Garage4Hackers Pune Meet 29-Dec-2012

    HI Members,

    Its time for Pune Garage4hackers Meet. We are assuming that world will not be ending on 21-Dec-2012 so join us for the Pune Garage4hackers Meet on

    Date : 29-Dec-2012
    Time : 11AM
    Venue : Viman Nagar / Kalyani Nager / FC college (Vote for it)

    Talks :
    No Talks!!!
    seriously this is the main attraction of our Meet. we dont do any presentations/seminars. we gather, discuss on various security topics, share ideas, raise questions(from N00bs to 31337 level), discuss various project, we even had some practical hacking session such as RFID evasion in Mall's previously etc. so feel free to join us. its free of cost only requirement is knowledge sharing attitude.

    For any questions feel free to PM.


    Thanks,
    41.w4r10r
