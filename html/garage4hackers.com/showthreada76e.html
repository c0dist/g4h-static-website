    In the latest episode of Hak5, Raphael Mudge of FastAndEasyHacking.com joins Rob Fuller, aka Mubix, to talk about his project Armitage; a cross-platform GUI front-end for Rapid7's Metasploit. Mudge demonstrate setting up the software, scanning for targets, attacking hosts with client side attacks or remote exploits, and finally pivoting throughout the network using pass-the-hash techniques. Time to grab some paper, pencil and an unsuspecting virtual machine!
    This video tutorial is excellent for beginners trying to set up Armitage in Backtrack R2.



    P.S. : At time 14:10 in the video,Mudge says that he is working to incorporate the features in Armitage, for generating automated reports !!
