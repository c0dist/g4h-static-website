    Go aka Golang - Next generation programming language from Google
    Hi Guys,

    Probably you are already aware of this, for those who are not aware of it. Go popularly known as Golang is a new programming language from Google.
    Development started around 2007 and the project was outsourced in 2009. IMO this would be the language to watch out for in the coming decade.
    Why you would ask ? We'll lets put it in the words of Golang's founders which also includes creator of Unix as well. Most languages that we use today
    were designed when computers were in their infancy. To handle todays multi-core, highly networked, concurrent system a new language was needed.
    Following are some of the links that might get you started. Thanks.

    http://tour.golang.org/#1

    https://code.google.com/p/go-wiki/wiki/GoUsers
