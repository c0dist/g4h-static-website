    Must Read: QnA Community Rules
    Hackers, we are glad to announce our new QnA portal for Hackers, you can access portal here, as you know we want everyone of you to participate and learn so, we have few simple rules that we will like you to support us with:

    1) We are not a Black Hat forum, so we neither do or support any such activity. Respect people's privacy.
    2) Use Google before asking simple questions, however you are always welcome to ask one. Don't make others Google it out for you.
    3) Do answer to a question if your have a knowledge of that particular topic. Sharing is caring.
    4) Don't request fellow hackers to hack an account or site for you.
    5) We believe knowledge should be open so, don't promote commercial solutions.

    We are excited to see everyone learn and grow.

    Let the games begin!
