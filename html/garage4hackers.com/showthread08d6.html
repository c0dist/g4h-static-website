    Few proposals for Garage

    Greeting to all!!

    Hope whoever attended Null had a gr8 time and networked with like minded ppl.I really wished I could attend and meet lot of my friends frm Garage there...but due to unavoidable circumstances tht meet still eludes me....


    I would like to put in few areas we need changes and all of your feedbacks to take.

    1) We have noticed recently that images uploaded with imagehack had been blocked and lot of our blogs and threads which had images are affected.I would request the respective blogger or thread starter to have a look at it and make necessary changes.
    This is very important coz we have some amazing blogs/articles and lets not disappoint visitors with incomplete data.

    2) We are currently looking for active moderators and administrators to run the forum.
    Most of us here are working professionals and we travel a lot,this has been a hindrance to dedicate time for the development of the forum.

    We would love to have a non-working/student/researchers in our team.The individual shud possess the passion and creativeness to run the forum.Please send in your nominations to info@garage4hackers{dot}com.

    3) Sometime back we started the initiative to design a homepage/logo and deploy it.Many of our members did a gud job and we shortlisted a design.http://vbtest.satarahost.co.cc/g4h/

    Please give me ur feedbacks,lets make necessary changes and deploy it.If we need to get a paid service to enhance it lets take tht option.

    4) We did implement a wiki for garage http://wiki.garage4hackers.com.

    The aim of the wiki is to help pentesters with recommendations/fixes for clients.
    Its a kind of database which has the writings/fixes for all kind of vulnerabilities reported.
    Or if anyone of use have a different look/idea to utilize this please drop in a mail or provide ur feedback here.

    Again we need volunteers/moderators to take up this project.

    As usual we love to listen to your views and ideas.....lets keep rocking & learning.

    And before I end I want to thank all who helped us in making Garage T-Shirts.
    Special thanks to the_empty,w4ri0r,fb1h2s and sorry if I missed any names.

    Cheers
    Anarki
