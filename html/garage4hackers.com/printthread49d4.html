    General Rules

    1. All posts must be written in English.
    2. Don't spam/abuse any other member via e-mail or Private Messages.

    6. No "Flaming". Please do not post any messages that harass, insult, belittle, threaten or flame another member or guest.
    7. No Offensive material. Do not post messages that are obscene, vulgar, sexually-orientated, hateful, threatening, racist, sexist, discriminatory, and/or otherwise violate of any local or international laws. This includes links in your signature, profile, posted images, photos and avatars. Staff reserves the right to decide if something is appropriate or not.
    8. Any religious, political or pornographic references will not be tolerated.
    9. Kindly post your technical queries in appropriate section only. Queries posted in another sections will not be entertained and will be removed.
    10. Don't use words such like "help", "urgent", "please" etc. for the subject. Use an appropriate and informative subjects.
    11. Kindly avoid pasting the links of pirated copies.
    12. Kindly tag your post as it makes easier for others to search it.
    13. Use Common sense. Just because something isn’t listed above doesn’t mean that you can do it.
    14. Using Common sense will help Quarantine you out of most trouble.
    15. Consequence: Varies
    16. DO not Post Any Live vulnerable targets in the Forum
