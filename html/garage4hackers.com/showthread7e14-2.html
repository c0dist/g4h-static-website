    Thats a very interesting Topic you put up Er3bus:

    There would be multiple opinions...

    My personal opinions is No matter How people are considering "Hackers" (Good or Bad) its always good to Keep you real life and online life seperate.
    There are multiple reasons for it

    1) You dont know who the exact person is if you have only met him online. It is very easy to pretend to be some one else in Online life.
    But that is not the case in Real life.

    2) Some times your talks / opinions in Online life might be considered bad by the people in Real life (Who are not hackers).

    3) You Carrier also might get impact if you tell about things you do online (even if they might be ethical). People in India still dont take Hacker / Hacking as good work.

    Till the time we hackers dont take initiative to build a good image for the word "Hacker" it would remain the same.

    So my personal opinion Keep your real life and online life seperate.
