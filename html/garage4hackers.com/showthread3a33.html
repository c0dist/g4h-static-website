    Dear Garage, Sorry And Thank You

    Hi friends......

    I know the title is little bit weird but that is what popping up in my mind from several days.

    I want to thank not one or two people, but this name, this place..... our Garage, Hackers Garage for each and everything that I have achieved in last year, both as an learner and as an professional.

    All those who are new to Garage and have joined after we became a Forum - Take my word on this, trust Garage and you surely will excel in this magical stream of hacking. Garage has taught me not only techniques, not only cool hacking stuff but to learn itself, to believe in myself and to remember one thing -
    Human knowledge belongs to the word. A good hacker always keeps sharing, he is a good seeder not just a leecher.

    And I want to convey my apologies to Garage and all its members for I am failing what I am supposed to do, rather what I always wanted to do. I am failing to share and take the responsibility of spreading this name which have brought so many dreams of mine to reality. I will surely return with some good stuff that I have learnt in past couple of months. And will try to keep learning and sharing.

    Tough I am away from this place, I just could not hide my emotions for Garage.

    LOVE YOU GARAGE
    ACCESS is GOD
