    APKs Backup
    1. Downloading APK, direct from your web browser: Don’t have working internet connection on phone? Need to download APK for backup purpose? Want to disassemble any APK? You can download APKs from your browser using this extension for Chrome, “APK Downloader ”.

    2. Copying installed apps’ APK (For rooted phones only): Want to have APKs of your already installed apps? But no internet connection? No worries, you’ve them in your phone only. Browse to “/data/app”, you may find many of them. Apps installed in SD card can be found at “/mnt/asec”.
    Don’t want to bother yourself? Try this app -> AntTek App Manager
